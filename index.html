Photography At a Glance

Photography is a combination of the technical and the artistic, and a majority of what makes a photo instagram-ready or what makes one feel something is beautiful comes down to a subjective sense of aesthetics and digital enhancement. Although the enhancement might be imperceptible, it can profoundly change the perception of an image. My general rule, though, is to do just that; enhance, but to keep in tact the natural beauty of the subject/s of my photos. So, especially today, digital enhancement has become the icing on the cake in photography that changes one photo from being mediocre to truly breathtaking, and some would say that without it, the one viewing will feel that something is missing. Altogether photography today is an exhilarating undertaking for anyone, and I do mean that--anyone. Learning basic composition can make even the most amateur of photo-taker into a seeming novice almost instantly.
